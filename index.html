FAQ

Is this an official ARC Raiders service?

No. It is a community-run trading service built for players who want more structure and less nonsense when making deals.

What can I trade here?

Any item in ARC Raiders is supported.

Why use RaiderTrader instead of a normal Discord channel?

Because listings, rep, middleman requests, drop-order logic, and trade tickets stay organized, visible, and easier to trust than random messages and DMs.
